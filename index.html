About me



Welcome to my world!

I am a second year student pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science & Design at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi. I aim to create by taking core design with technology and integrating them into one final design. Inquisitive by nature, I love to observe the environment around me and think of solutions to existing problems. My areas of interest are Product Design and Graphic Design. I'm also currently working as a designer at GirlUp Udaan IIITD which is a part of the global movement started by the UN.
As an automobile enthusiast, Automobile Design happens to be my passion.
